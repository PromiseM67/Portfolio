I am
Promise.

I’m an international student from South Africa with a deep curiosity for how things work, especially technology. I’m naturally ambitious, always asking questions, always trying to understand systems beyond the surface.I don’t like consuming experiences. I like building them.
I’m still early in my journey. Right now, I’m focused on front-end development and learning how to create websites that don’t just function, but feel intentional. At the same time, I’m stepping into backend development to deepen my understanding of how systems work behind the scenes. I want to go beyond surface-level interfaces and build complete, scalable applications from end to end. I’m experimenting, refining, and building projects that push my creativity while sharpening my technical foundation so I can grow, not just as a developer but as an individual.
I’ve developed a strong interest in UI/UX and graphic design. I’m learning how to integrate design thinking into my front-end work so that every interaction has purpose. My goal isn’t just to write code but to design experiences that are visually compelling, intuitive, and memorable.
I see myself building a career in the tech industry, not just as a developer, but as someone who blends creativity and technical skill. This is still the beginning. I’m learning. I’m experimenting. I’m building. And I’m only getting started.
